About

Home Page

The Marvel Cinematic Universe includes a series of interconnected stories and characters that have been built up since 2008. I am fascinated by how the several story arcs were created over a decade ago, ultimately bringing together the entire set of characters in the final two Avengers movies. Many of the early films included Easter Eggs that provided clues into future movies, characters, and plot lines to come. This has created a set of fans, including myself, who attempt to predict upcoming storylines and eagerly await sequels and new character introductions. I am particularly fascinated by the MCU because, due to the films' interconnectivity, each movie not only pushes the development of its protagonist but the overall narrative of the entire franchise.
