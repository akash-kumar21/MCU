What If...



Date: November 3, 2020

Author: Akash Kumar

If you are focused on the upcoming movie releases in the Marvel Universe, don’t forget the pretty exciting lineup of TV shows on Disney+. And we are talking about some pretty big budget, tent pole shows. One of the most anticipated shows will release in the summer of 2021. The show is mysteriously called WhatIf…? will explore alternate versions of iconic scenes and moments in the MCU.

Marvel has also lined up an impressive array of actors from the universe to play a role in the new series. These include Michael B. Jordan, Sebastian Stan, Josh Brolin, Mark Ruffalo, Tom Hiddleston, Samuel L. Jackson, Chris Hemsworth, Hayley Atwell, Chadwick Boseman, Karen Gillan, Jeremy Renner, Paul Rudd, Michael Douglas, Neal McDonough, Dominic Cooper, Sean Gunn, Nicole Portman, Stanley Tucci, Taika Waititi, Toby Jones, Djimon Honsou, Michael Rooker, and Jeff Goldblum.
