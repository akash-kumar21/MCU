Loki



Date: November 5, 2020

Author: Akash Kumar

He’s back! The bad guy that somehow is likeable, AKA Loki is back in the Spring of 2021 on Disney+. The show got a 3-second teaser during the Super Bowl that didn’t give too much away but got fans wanting more. Tom Hiddleston promises that we get to know what he does with the Space Stone after his surprise disappearance at the end of Avengers: Endgame. And Kevin Feige has confirmed that the show connects to the upcoming movie, Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness. Some leaked photos from shooting also hint at the possibility of a Lady Loki played by Sophia Di Martino.

Other actors joining the show include Owen Wilson and Gugu Mbatha-Raw although their characters in the show are yet to be confirmed. What we do know however is that, thanks to the popularity of Loki, a Season 2 has already been commissioned -- even before finishing filming on Season 1. As Loki himself said, “There are no men like me.”
