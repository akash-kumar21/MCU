Spiderverse!!!



Date: November 1, 2020

Author: Akash Kumar

There was an idea to bring together a group of remarkable people... three spidermen! The lineup for MCU's to-be-titled Spider-Man 3 is looking CRAZY. Jamie Foxx is returning as Electro, Doctor Strange will be included, and now Tobey Macguire and Andrew Garfield are rumored to reprise their roles as Spider-Man in the MCU, joining current Spider-Man Tom Holland.

Fans have been pleading for a live-action Spider-Verse for years, and it now it may finally happen. I think it would be really cool, and it could work really well with the narrative since Doctor Strange could bring Tobey Macguire and Andrew Garfield in using his magical powers.

That said, I am actually not a huge fan of the idea of Doctor Strange being in the film since I’ve heard his purpose is to act as a sort of mentor for Spider-Man in the way that Iron Man was prior. I think it’s time for Spider-Man to grow out of this need for a mentor, and perhaps even take on a larger role on the Avengers.
